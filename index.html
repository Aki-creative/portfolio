About Me

I was born in Osaka, Japan. I graduated from Osaka University of Arts in 2013, majoring in visual arts and graphic design. ​ During my last summer vacation, I journeyed through various parts of South East Asia as a conception for my art work. ​ Since 2015, I have been living in Brisbane, Australia studying Graphic Design at TAFE Queensland due to the achievement to become an artist in Australia. After graduated the TAFE at end of 2016, I started working as Graphic designer in Brisbane. Currently, I am a freelance graphic designer in Melbourne. Beside working, I also create art works which comprises of hand drawing and computer graphic design for paper media.

I would gladly applicate if you show interests in me after looking through my profile. I believe that I am able to create something unique that can't be expressed solely through photography and drawing. ​ I believe that the combination of fine strokes and vibrant colors are the way to fascinate and capture people's heart. ​
